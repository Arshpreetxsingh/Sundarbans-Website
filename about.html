About Sundarbans House

Why It Was Created

Sundarbans exists so students don’t feel like they’re studying alone in an online program. You are part of a house, a real community, not a database entry.

Regional Meetups

Plan trips, discover new places, meet offline, hang out, and make unforgettable memories through fun activities together.

Regional Groups

Every region has its own group, making it easy for students to connect with nearby communities, interact with others, and grow their network.

Events & Engagement

Contests, hackathons, challenges, club activities. Learn + contribute + grow.

Peer Support & Diversity

A mix of different experiences, cultures, and skills. Students support each other, share insights, and build a community where everyone feels included.

"Sundarbans House creates a space for connection, collaboration, and community.”
